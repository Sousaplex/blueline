# Strangeloop Coffee — brand style guide

## Palette
- Espresso brown `#2B1D16` — primary text, headers
- Warm cream `#F5EEE4` — backgrounds
- Copper `#C1673C` — accents, CTA, rules/dividers
- Deep teal `#1F4A46` — secondary accent, sparingly

## Typography
- Headlines: high-contrast serif (Georgia or similar system serif), tight leading
- Body: humanist sans (Helvetica Neue / system sans), 9.5–11pt for print
- Never use more than 2 typefaces

## Layout principles
- Generous margins (≥14mm), strong single-column or asymmetric two-column grids
- Copper rules (1pt) to separate sections, never boxes with borders
- Images bleed to page edge on at most one side

## Imagery
- Warm, natural light; hands, texture, steam; shallow depth of field
- No stock-photo gloss, no latte-art clichés as the hero
