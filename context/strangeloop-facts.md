# Strangeloop Coffee — facts sheet (source material)

- Founded 2019 in Portland, OR by Maya Chen (former Q-grader) and Tom Okafor.
- Roasts Tuesdays and Fridays on a 15kg Loring Smart Roast; ~40 wholesale partners.
- Farm partners: 4 in Ethiopia (Yirgacheffe, Guji), 3 in Colombia (Huila), 2 in Guatemala (Antigua).
- Publishes a yearly transparency report with FOB prices paid per lot.
- Wholesale program: no minimums for the first 3 months, free on-site barista training
  (2 sessions/quarter), seasonal menu consulting, 48-hour delivery in the Pacific NW.
- Cupping room open every Thursday 10am; bookings via hello@strangeloop.coffee.
- 2025: Good Food Award finalist (Guji natural lot #14).
