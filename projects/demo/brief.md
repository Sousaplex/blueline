# Brief: Strangeloop Coffee — wholesale one-pager

**Format:** one-pager, A4 portrait, print (PDF)
**Audience:** specialty cafe owners and head baristas deciding on a wholesale roaster
**Goal:** get them to book a cupping session

## Key messages
1. Strangeloop roasts in small batches, twice a week — cafes never serve coffee older than 10 days off roast.
2. Direct relationships with 9 farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala; transparent pricing published yearly.
3. Wholesale partners get free barista training and seasonal menu consulting.

## Must include
- Headline + 2–3 short supporting sections (no walls of text)
- One hero image (roastery or coffee, warm and craft-feeling)
- One smaller accent image
- Call to action: "Book a cupping — hello@strangeloop.coffee"
- Company name and tagline: "Strangeloop Coffee — Roasted in loops, poured with intent"

## Tone
Confident, warm, craft-driven. Not corporate, not twee.
