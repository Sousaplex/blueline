# Brief: Strangeloop Coffee — spring seasonal menu poster

**Format:** poster, A3 portrait, print (PDF)
**Audience:** walk-in cafe customers of wholesale partner shops
**Goal:** make people order the spring seasonal drinks

## Key messages
1. Three spring drinks: Yuzu Honey Latte, Guji Natural pour-over (Good Food Award finalist), Cold Fog (oat cold brew with lavender mist).
2. Available March–May only, at participating partner cafes.
3. Beans from the new Huila harvest, landed in February.

## Must include
- One dominant hero visual, minimal copy — poster reads from 3 meters away
- The three drink names with one-line descriptions
- "Ask your barista" call to action
- Strangeloop logo text + tagline

## Tone
Fresh, bright, still craft — spring light rather than cozy winter.
