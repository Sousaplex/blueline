Consolidating EDC, eSource, and eTMF into a Single GxP-Validated Clinical Ecosystem

Protocol AI & Unified Workflows

Clincove consolidates clinical operations by integrating Electronic Data Capture (EDC), eSource (SMR), and document management (eTMF & eISF) into a single, seamless platform. Its intelligent Protocol AI module reads study protocols to automatically generate compliance-ready Case Report Forms (CRFs), visit checklists, and budgets—slashing setup times from weeks to hours and avoiding disconnected legacy vendors.

Uncompromising HIPAA Scope Isolation

Designed for secure data-plane execution, Clincove enforces multi-tenant security and strict patient-level isolation on all clinical databases. Backed by the robust StrangeLoop engine, the platform implements a deny-by-default architecture where records for specific studies (e.g., Study SL-TEST-A, Patient P1) are strictly isolated from sibling records or foreign studies, ensuring absolute patient privacy.

Point-of-Care eSource Synchronization

By capturing Source Medical Records (SMR) digitally at the point of care during patient visits, Clincove streams structured, timestamped data directly into the EDC. This completely eliminates manual transcription steps, prevents up to 3,200 data discrepancies per trial, and accelerates database locks using automated AI-driven queries and integrated medical coding (MedDRA and WHODrug).

Clincove cuts average study deployment from 12 weeks to 2-4 weeks, launching first patient visits rapidly with zero upfront infrastructure costs.
